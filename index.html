Our Vision

Device to help partially able individuals to exercise

At Disability Rehabilitation, we're committed to developing innovative solutions that empower individuals with partial mobility to maintain active lifestyles and improve their quality of life.

Exercise your limbs in supine, sitting and standing position

Our revolutionary device is designed to accommodate various body positions, allowing users to exercise effectively regardless of their mobility limitations.

Supine Position

Exercise comfortably while lying down, reducing strain on the back and providing support for users with limited trunk control.

Sitting Position

Engage in effective workouts from a seated position, perfect for wheelchair users or those who find standing difficult.

Standing Position

Gradually build strength and stability with supported standing exercises designed for progressive rehabilitation.
